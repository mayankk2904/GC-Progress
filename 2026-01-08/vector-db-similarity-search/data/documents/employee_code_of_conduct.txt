Employee Code of Conduct

1. Professional Behavior
Employees must conduct themselves in a professional manner at all times. Harassment,
discrimination, or abusive behavior will not be tolerated.

2. Conflict of Interest
Employees must avoid situations where personal interests conflict with company interests.
Any potential conflicts must be disclosed to management.

3. Confidentiality
Employees must not disclose confidential company information to external parties without
proper authorization.

4. Workplace Ethics
Bribery, corruption, or unethical business practices are strictly prohibited.

5. Disciplinary Action
Violation of this code may result in disciplinary action including termination of employment.
