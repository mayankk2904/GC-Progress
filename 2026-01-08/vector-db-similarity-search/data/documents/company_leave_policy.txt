Company Leave Policy

1. Purpose
The purpose of this leave policy is to provide employees with time off from work for rest,
personal matters, illness, and other life events while ensuring smooth business operations.

2. Annual Leave
Employees are entitled to 24 days of paid annual leave per calendar year. Annual leave
accrues monthly and must be approved by the reporting manager. Unused leave may be
carried forward up to a maximum of 10 days.

3. Sick Leave
Employees are entitled to 12 days of sick leave per year. Sick leave can be used in case
of illness or medical emergencies. Medical certificates are required for absences exceeding
two consecutive working days.

4. Casual Leave
Casual leave of up to 6 days per year may be taken for personal matters. Casual leave
cannot be carried forward to the next year.

5. Maternity Leave
Female employees are entitled to 26 weeks of paid maternity leave as per government
regulations. Adoption leave of 12 weeks is also provided.

6. Paternity Leave
Male employees are entitled to 10 working days of paternity leave, which must be used
within three months of childbirth.

except in emergencies.
