Information Security Policy

1. Objective
This policy defines the guidelines for protecting company information assets from
unauthorized access, disclosure, modification, or destruction.

2. Access Control
Access to company systems must be granted based on the principle of least privilege.
Employees must not share passwords or authentication credentials with anyone.

3. Data Classification
Company data is classified into Public, Internal, Confidential, and Restricted categories.
Restricted data must be encrypted both at rest and in transit.

4. Acceptable Use
Company systems must only be used for authorized business purposes. Installing
unauthorized software is strictly prohibited.

5. Incident Management
Any suspected security incident must be reported to the IT security team within
24 hours. Failure to report incidents may result in disciplinary action.

6. Remote Work Security
Employees working remotely must use company-approved VPNs and ensure devices
are protected with updated antivirus software.

7. Compliance
All employees must comply with this policy. Non-compliance may lead to termination
or legal action.
